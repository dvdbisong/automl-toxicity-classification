Slovak Wikipedians notice board
Hi You might be interested in checking out the brand new WikipediaSlovak Wikipedians notice board All Wikipedians interested in Slovakia or subjects somehow related to Slovakia are invited to use this noticeboard as a tool for better organizing their activities making announcements to others with similar interests and discussing any actual issues related to their work on Wikipedia You are welcome to add this notice board to your watchlist so that you could see when it is updated and thus take part in any projects initiatives and improvements relating to articles about Slovakia Also you are welcome to report your own articles here so they could be peerreviewed and expanded by other contributors
