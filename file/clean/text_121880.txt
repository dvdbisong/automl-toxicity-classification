A lot of the historic buildings are on Pearl Street in Boulders downtown  Theres a fourblock pedestrian mall on Pearl Street between 11th and 15th Streets that features a lot of historic buildings and there are plaques that provide historic interpretation  The mall and other sections of Pearl Street also has several interesting shops street performers outdoor concerts and the like  It was enough to keep a tourist like me entertained  If you ever go back there again check it out   Elkspeak
