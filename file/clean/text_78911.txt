Gwiazda Polish for Star was a weekly newspaper published in Philadelphia Pennsylvania in Polish from 1902 to 1985 with an English section gradually introduced starting in 1958  The founder and publisher was the Polish born Stephan Nowaczyk a printer who immigrated to Philadelphia in the 1870s and started the newspaper in his home in Port Richmond with used equipment in 1902  When he died in 1923 his wife Francis became the publisher until 1935 when their daughter Gertrude became the last publisher until 1985 when the paper was dissolved
