Fullerton Financial Holdings Pte Ltd invests in financial institutions in emerging markets bringing an operational perspective to all investment decisions Fullerton Financial Holdings Pte Ltd supervises and influences its banks to achieve the right riskreward balance Primarily it focuses on both Business banking and Consumer banking Within Business banking Fullerton Financial Holdings Pte Ltd focuses on the Commercial SME and Selfemployed mass market segments On the other hand it focuses on the Mass affluent and Mass salaried segments within Consumer banking Fullerton Financial Holdings Pte Ltd is a wholly owned subsidiary of Temasek Holdings an Asia investment house headquartered in Singapore
