The local governors of Lelang commandery when Choi Ri governed the Nangnang State
At that time the local governor of Lelang commandery was 王調 and 유헌劉憲 Thus it is obvious that the king Choi Ri is different person from Wangjo and Yuheon Consequently the Nangnang State is different from the Lelang commandery In addition Book of Later Han refer to the governor of the Lelang commandery as 太守 not king
